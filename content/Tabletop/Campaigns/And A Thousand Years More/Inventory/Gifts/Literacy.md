> #Gifts/Keystone

> [!INFO] You can read!
> - You can read and write.
> 	- The DM can call for rolls of *Mind* (`dice: 1d6`) and *Academics* (`dice: 1d1`) to understand particularly difficult texts.
> 	- Characters without this *Gift* cannot read or write at all.
